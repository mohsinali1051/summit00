TheCryptoHub (TCH) Summit is a flagship event in Dubai, which has positioned itself as the global hub for Cryptos & Web and attracted a global pool of talent to this remarkable city. Why Join The Summit

Pitches

The summit provides a platform for curated start-up founders to pitch their projects in front of Angel Investors, VC Firms & the Web3 community.

Panels

Participate in excellent panel discussions on the hottest current topics in Cryptos & Web3 to discuss the latest in DeFI, Metaverse, Gamefi & NFTs

Networking

A great opportunity to network with Influencers, founders & investors in a 5-star venue in Dubai providing excellent lunches & refreshments.

Investors

Find the best curated projects which undergo our due diligene & vetting before joining our select list of start-ups.

Founders

Pitch your projects to the top Angel Investors and Venture Capital (VCs) firms specialised in Cryptos & Web3.

Community

Bringing the Crypto & Web3 community together and helping build the ecosystem of The Crypto Hub for all to benefit & thrive in a culture of collaboration.
